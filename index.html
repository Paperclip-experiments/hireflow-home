Frequently asked questions

What is HireFlow?
HireFlow is an AI-powered client acquisition and pipeline management platform built specifically for SMB staffing and recruitment agencies. It helps you find companies with active hiring needs, automate BD outreach, manage your client pipeline, and deliver candidates through branded portals.
How is HireFlow different from an ATS?
An ATS manages candidates. HireFlow manages clients. Most staffing tools focus on sourcing, screening, and placement tracking. HireFlow focuses on the business development side — finding new clients, automating outreach, and managing your client pipeline. It works alongside your existing ATS, not as a replacement.
Does HireFlow replace Bullhorn?
No. HireFlow integrates with Bullhorn, JobAdder, Recruit CRM, and other ATS platforms. It adds the client acquisition and BD automation layer that most ATS platforms lack. Think of it as your BD engine that feeds clients into your existing recruiting workflow.
How does AI prospecting work?
HireFlow monitors job boards, funding announcements, and company growth data to identify companies with active hiring intent. Each prospect is scored and ranked by urgency, so your BD team can focus on the companies most likely to convert right now.
How long does setup take?
Most agencies are up and running within a day. Connect your email, import your existing prospects (optional), and start discovering new ones immediately. No engineering required.
Is my data secure?
Yes. HireFlow uses bank-grade encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II compliance, and GDPR-compliant data handling. Your client data is isolated per account and never shared.
